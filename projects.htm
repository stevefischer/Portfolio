Guitars Galore

The first website that I created was for my senior project as part of the Bachelor's of Applied Science in Information Systems (abbreviated BAS-IS) program while I attended Olympic College in Spring of 2018.

I chose this as my project because of my enjoyment of playing guitar. I have played guitar for sixteen years now and over the years I have gained a lot of knowledge about many makes and models and that is what the intent of the website is showing.

The website is an information site about five different models of guitars, as well as some essential accessories every guitarist needs to have in order to be successful when playing guitar. If you are looking into wanting to learn or purchasing a guitar of your own, the site also includes a "Resources" page giving the links to the most popular sites to look at for reference.

You can view the final project at this link.
